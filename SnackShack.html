Snack Shack - Coming of Age

Set in the summer of 1991 in small town Nebraska, best friends AJ and Moose decide to buy and run the local pool's rundown snack shack in hopes of making money. Throughout the summer, they meet newcomer lifeguard, Brooke, engage in neighborhood rivalries, and face personal growth and loss.

When to Watch:

  • friend group sleepover on a warm summer night (bonus points for smores!)
  • background movie while preparing for a lemonade stand to motivate the hustle
